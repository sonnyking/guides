Pixel Perfection for the web
Photoshop style guides in your browser, a light-weight little diddy to help make your HTML all pretty.

Give'em a try, just type [r] and [g] to show or hide'em. Click and drag from one of the rulers to place your guide. You can always move'em again by hovering until you get a cursor and drag.
